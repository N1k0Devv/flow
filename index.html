Common Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Is Flow only for people with ADHD?

No — Flow is designed with ADHD brains in mind, but anyone who wants to do deep work without distractions benefits from it. The gamification and audio cues work for everyone who struggles to stay on task.

What platforms is Flow available on?

Currently Windows 11 desktop only. The app is built with Electron for a native experience. macOS and mobile versions are on the roadmap.

How does the free trial work?

You get 7 days of full Pro access — no credit card required. After the trial, you choose a plan or stay on the free tier with basic features.

Does Google Calendar sync work automatically?

Yes. Once you connect your Google account in Settings → Integrations, any task you schedule on the calendar syncs to Google Calendar instantly. Changes in Google Calendar also pull back into Flow.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your data is always yours. We store everything securely in the cloud and you can export it at any time. Cancelling only removes Pro features — your tasks and history stay intact.

Can I use Flow offline?

Yes. The app works fully offline — tasks, timer, and habits all work without internet. Your data syncs automatically the next time you connect.
